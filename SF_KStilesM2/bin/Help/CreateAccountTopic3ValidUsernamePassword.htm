USERNAME REQUIREMENTS:
  • Username must be unique and cannot be repeated for any other user
  • Usernames are not case-sensitive
  • Cannot begin with a number
  • Cannot be less than 8 characters long
  • Cannot be longer than 20 characters
  • Cannot contain special characters
  • Cannot contain, start with or end with spaces
PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS:
  • Password must contain AT LEAST THREE of the four types to be valid password
    1. Uppercase character (A-Z)
    2. Lowercase character (a-z)
    3. Number (0-9)
    4. Special character (()!@#$%^&*)
  • Case-sensitive
  • Cannot be less than 8 characters long
  • Cannot be longer than 20 characters
